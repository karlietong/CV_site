What's a riboswitch? A riboswitch is a piece of mRNA that can bind to a ligand, in my team's case, this ligand was fluoride. When fluoride does bind, the riboswitch is activated, leading to gene transcription.

The harmful effects of fluoride
Fluoride stimulating our riboswitch

Why is excess fluoride an issue? Although fluoride has a positive association in dental products, too much can lead to severe conditions such as, dental flurosis (when fluoride intake reaches 10mg) and skeletal flurosis (daily intake of 6mg). In 1991, the World Health Organization has reported endemic fluorsis in 28 provinces of China and 13 territories of India. Sri Lanka also found concentrations of up to 10mg in groundwater (WHO, 2006).
